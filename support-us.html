Can we support each other?

The Peruvian Association of Atheists is one of the few organizations dedicated to the struggle for the achievement of a secular state in Peru, a country by tradition and confessional praxis. We support projects, events and groups that spread an atheist, naturalist and scientistic worldview. In addition, we legally defend and guide non-believers when their right to conscientious objection is affected. If you feel identified with our work, your support is very important to us. The proceeds will be used in actions such as:

  • Creation and development of projects in favor of secularism in Peru.
  • Maintenance of the Legal Defense area of APERAT.
  • Coordinate meetings with different groups related to our ideas.
  • Dissemination of secular humanism.
  • Meetings with atheist people from different parts of the country.

ABOUT US

APERAT is an entity registered in the Public Registry as a non-profit association registered in the Public Registry under the Record No. 12658791. As an organization, APERAT brings together and provides spaces for action and dialogue to the community of non-believers and secular humanists in Peru. APERAT is part of Humanists International.
